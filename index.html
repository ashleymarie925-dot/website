Common Questions

Answers before you even ask.

How much house can I afford?
Lending guidelines generally allow a debt-to-income ratio of up to 50% to 55%, but everyone's situation and comfort level is different. The most important part is that you are comfortable with your monthly payment given your expenses and your lifestyle. That is exactly what we focus on and build a plan around together.
What credit score do I need to buy a house?
Conventional loans typically start at 620, while FHA and VA loans can accept lower scores. Even if your score is not there yet, I can help you build a plan to get mortgage-ready.
How much down payment do I need?
It can be less than you think. VA loans offer $0 down, FHA starts at 3.5%, and conventional options start at 3%. Down payment assistance programs may also be available.
Should I refinance my mortgage?
It depends on your current rate, how long you plan to stay, and your goals: lowering payments, pulling cash out, or paying off faster. I will run the numbers with you so the decision is clear.
